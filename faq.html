FAQ

NO BS IF
THE MODEL
Is the app really free forever?

Yes. Core functionality is free with no expiry date, no trial period, and no account required. The app that does what it says on the tin is the free version. That's the whole point.

Is there a subscription?

No. There is a one-time in-app purchase for extras — themes, widgets, and anything we add in future. You pay once. You own it. We will not introduce a subscription tier later. That's a commitment, not a marketing line.

What does the one-time unlock include?

All 7 additional themes (ICE, MOSS, SOIL, GLAM, SOLAR, CRIMSON, GRAPHITE), the home screen widget, and any themes or features we release in the future. One purchase, no renewals, no tiers.

Will you add ads later?

No. Ads are incompatible with the product we're building. The business model is one-time purchases. That's it.

What platforms do you support?

iOS and Android. We ship on both. There's no web app — NO BS IF is a native mobile app.

PRIVACY
Do you collect any of my data?

Nothing. No analytics, no crash reporting, no advertising identifiers, no third-party tracking SDKs. We have no idea how many people use the app or how they use it. This is a deliberate choice, not an oversight.

Full details in our privacy policy.

Do I need an account?

No. No sign-up, no email, no login. You open the app and it works.

Where is my data stored?

On your device. Nowhere else. We don't have servers storing your fasting history because we don't need them — the app works entirely offline.

What happens to my data if I uninstall?

It's gone with the app. Export your data first if you want to keep it or move it to another device. You'll find the export option in Settings.

HOW IT WORKS
What fasting protocols does it support?

You set your own target duration. 16:8, 18:6, 24 hours, anything — you decide. The app is a timer, not a protocol enforcer. It doesn't care which method you use.

What if I forgot to start the timer?

Fix It mode lets you set any start time in the past. Forgot to tap when you started? Set it to when you actually started and the timer catches up.

Does it sync across devices?

No cloud sync. Your data stays on your device. To move to a new phone, export from the old one and import on the new one. You're in control of where your data goes.

How does the streak work?

Every calendar day you complete a fast counts toward your streak. Miss a day and it resets. No partial credit, no grace periods — an honest count.

Can I export my data?

Yes. Export your full history as a file from Settings. Import it again on any device running NO BS IF. Your data, your format, no lock-in.

SUPPORT
How do I report a bug?

Email us. Tell us what happened, what you expected, and what device and OS version you're on. A real person reads every message.

Do you take feature requests?

Yes, via email. We can't promise we'll build everything, but we read everything. If a lot of people ask for the same thing and it fits the philosophy, it gets considered.
