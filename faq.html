FAQ

Last updated April 18, 2026 at 4:06 PM UTC
THE MODEL
Is the app really free forever?

Yes. The app's core functionality is free with no expiry date, no trial period, and no account required. The app is fully functional and you can use it forever without every paying anything.

Is there a subscription?

Nope. There never will be a subscription. There is a one-time in-app purchase for extras and that's it. This is a commitment, not a marketing line.

What does this one-time unlock include?
  • Home screen widget
  • All of the extra app themes
  • All additional themes we release
  • Warm fuzzy feeling of supporting an indie developer
Will you add ads later?

No. Ads are incompatible with our core principles and values. The business model is one-time purchases. That's it. Check out our manifesto to learn more about what we stand for.

What platforms do you support?

iOS and Android. We ship on both. There's no web app — NO BS IF is strictly a native mobile app.

PRIVACY
Do you collect any of my data?

Nothing. No analytics, no crash reporting, no advertising identifiers, no third-party tracking SDKs. We have no idea how many people use the app or how they use it. This is a deliberate choice, not an oversight.

Full details in our privacy policy.

Do I need an account?

No. No sign-up, no email, no login. You open the app and it works. If you make a purchase it is tied to the iOS or Android app store account you used.

Where is my data stored?

On your device. Nowhere else. We don't have servers storing your fasting history because we don't need them — the app works entirely offline. You can import and export your data at any time.

What happens to my data if I uninstall?

It's gone with the app. Export your data first if you want to keep it or move it to another device. You can find this in the app Settings page.

HOW IT WORKS
What fasting protocols does it support?

You set your own target duration. 16:8, 12:12 or anything in between up to 24 hours, anything — you decide. The app is a timer, not a protocol enforcer.

What if I forgot to start the timer?

No worries! You can edit the fast start time before, during or even after each fast.

Does it sync across devices?

No cloud sync. Your data stays on your device. To move to a new phone, use the export and import tools in Settings. You're in control of your data.

How does the streak work?

Every calendar day you complete a fast counts toward your streak. Miss a day and it resets. No partial credit and an optional, configurable grace period.

Can the app really tell if I am in ketosis?

No.It can't! The labels displayed are estimates based on general scientific literature. We have no access to any of your health data.

SUPPORT
How do I report a bug?

Email us. Tell us what happened, what you expected, and what device and OS version you're on. A real person reads every message.

Do you take feature requests?

Yes, via email. We can't promise we'll build everything, but we read everything. If a lot of people ask for the same thing and it fits the philosophy, it gets considered.
